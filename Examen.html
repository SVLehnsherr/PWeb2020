The mythical Billy returns, the fish who became so famous because he sings and moves in a super fun way. Now he's back with his one an only rendition of Gloria Gaynor's hit song "I will survive." When you push the button on the license plate, Billy starts singing the song "I will survive" and moves as he sings it. In addition, at the back you can activate the motion sensor which will make when someone passes in front of Billy, star singing.
